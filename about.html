About

I'm Ryan McComb — a student at Evanston Township High School interested in data, prediction markets, elections, and politics en general.

I'm a Student Fellow at VoteHub, where I contribute to decision-desk calls on primary night, work on prediction market data and modeling, and build election data pipelines. I also write internal and external analysis on races and results.

I built IL9Cast, a forecasting aggregator for the Illinois 9th Congressional District primary that synthesises prediction market odds, polling, and fundraising data. It's been covered in Capitol Fax, the Evanston Roundtable, and the Daily Northwestern.

Outside of elections, I'm a staff writer and photographer at The Evanstonian, a competitive swimmer, host of the Project 2028 Podcast, and a credentialled sports photographer for the Chicago Union.

Based in Evanston, Illinois.
