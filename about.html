I’m a student at Evanston Township High School. I work on data, prediction markets, and elections.

I’m a Student Fellow at VoteHub, doing decision-desk race calls on primary nights, modeling prediction market data, and building election data pipelines.

I built IL9Cast, a forecasting aggregator for the Illinois 9th Congressional District primary. It pulls together prediction market odds, polling, and fundraising data. It was covered in Capitol Fax, the Evanston Roundtable, and the Daily Northwestern.

I write and photograph for The Evanstonian and host the Project 2028 podcast (politics and policy).
