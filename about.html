I'm a student at Evanston Township High School interested in data, prediction markets, and elections.

As a Student Fellow at VoteHub, I contribute to decision-desk race calls on primary night, model prediction market data, and build election data pipelines.

I built IL9Cast, a forecasting aggregator for the Illinois 9th Congressional District primary that synthesises prediction market odds, polling, and fundraising data. It's been covered in Capitol Fax, the Evanston Roundtable, and the Daily Northwestern.

I also write and photograph for The Evanstonian and host the Project 2028 Podcast.
