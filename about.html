About Ryan McComb

Ryan
McComb

I’m a sophomore at Evanston Township High School who thinks about elections, prediction markets, and data more than is probably healthy.

I’m a Student Fellow at VoteHub, where I work on elections, decision desk coverage, data analysis, and a smidge of (subpar) modelling.

I built IL9Cast, a forecasting aggregator for the IL-9 Democratic primary that synthesises Kalshi and Manifold odds, polling, and FEC fundraising data into a single probabilistic model. It got covered by Capitol Fax, the Evanston Roundtable, and the Daily Northwestern.

I sit on the editorial board of The Evanstonian and occasionally write and photograph for them. I host the Project 2028 podcast and served as Volunteer Finance Lead on the Daniel Biss congressional campaign.

Resume if you want the formal CV (print or PDF). I also post online as @RyanJMcComb.
