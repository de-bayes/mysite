I'm Ryan McComb, a student at Evanston Township High School interested in data, prediction markets, politics, and the web. I like making things people can use and forming beliefs based on evidence.

I built IL9.org, an open-source political forecasting project. I'm also a Student Fellow at VoteHub doing race-call work and election modeling, and a Volunteer Finance Lead for the Biss congressional campaign.

I trade on play-money prediction markets, write essays on politics and data, and shoot film photography. My work has been featured in the Evanston Roundtable, the Daily Northwestern, and Capitol Fax.
